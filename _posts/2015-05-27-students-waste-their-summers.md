---
title: Students Waste Their Summers
layout: post
---

Lets say you're the typical honors student (I suppose that's safe to assume since you're reading this blog), you work your butt off all year to get "good" grades to get into that "decent" college and then--what, get a job? join the workforce of hundreds of thousands of zombie-like adults who rise much too early and sleep far too late?

Students cram all their learning into the school year in order to get a good grade, and it doesn't do anything for them in the long run. <b>Students waste their summers.</b>

Perhaps instead of spending five hours trying to learn how to, hell - what do they even learn these days? - learn how to program. That's something that you can at least put on your job application and increase your appeal over the competition.
